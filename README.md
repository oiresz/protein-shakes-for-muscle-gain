## What Are Protein Shakes?

Protein shakes are beverages made by mixing protein powder with water, milk, or other liquids. They provide a quick and efficient way to increase your daily protein intake, which is essential for muscle growth.

Common ingredients in protein shakes include:

- Whey protein
- Casein protein
- Plant-based protein (pea, soy, rice)
- Milk or almond milk
- Fruits and healthy fats (optional)

## How to Make a Protein Shake for Muscle Gain

Here’s a simple and effective protein shake recipe:

**Basic Muscle Gain Shake:**
- 1 scoop protein powder  
- 1 cup milk or water  
- 1 banana  
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter  

Blend all ingredients until smooth. This provides protein, carbs, and healthy fats for optimal muscle growth.
